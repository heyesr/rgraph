A Line chart showing the new (version 5.22) formatted tooltips feature

Formatted tooltips are a new feature in version 5.22 and make it super-easy to have unique tooltips but using just one specification in your configuration (as shown below).

[No canvas support] [No canvas support]

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.common.dynamic.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.common.tooltips.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.hprogress.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<canvas id="cvs1" width="150" height="650">[No canvas support]</canvas>
<canvas id="cvs2" width="150" height="650">[No canvas support]</canvas>
