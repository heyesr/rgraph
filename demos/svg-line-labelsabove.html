An SVG Line chart using the labelsAbove option

Here we have a Line chart that has the labelsAbove option enabled. The labelsAbove labels can be the values for the chart or they can also be specific bits of text that you supply.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.line.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="width: 750px; height: 300px" id="chart-container"></div>
