An SVG Bar chart showing the UK inflation rate

This is a copy of a chart that represents the UK monthly inflation rate (these are the real figures). Not good if you have a mortgage - but on the other hand if you're a saver its a good sign! It's using gradients, the labelsAbove option and a custom font. It also sets the yaxisScaleMax property to 3.5 so that the granularity is clearer.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.bar.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="width: 750px; height: 300px" id="chart-container"></div>
