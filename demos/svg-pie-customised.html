A customised SVG Pie chart

A Pie chart that's using segment separation and the roundRobin() effect. The SVG tag is quite wide in order to accommodate the labels.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.tooltips.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.pie.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="width: 850px; height: 350px" id="chart-container"></div>
