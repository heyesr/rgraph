An SVG Horizontal Bar chart showing late excuse numbers

A Horizontal Bar chart with a large left margin setting. With Horizontal Bar charts the left margin setting is auto-sized by default to make sufficient room for the labels. The chart is animated using the grow() effect and the labelsAbove option is enabled.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.hbar.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="padding: 15px">
    <div style="width: 750px; height: 500px" id="chart-container"></div>
</div>
