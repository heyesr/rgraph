An SVG Rose chart with unequal segment sizes

An example of the Rose chart where the segment sizes are unequal - thus allowing you to show an extra dimension of information.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.tooltips.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.rose.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="width: 650px; height: 500px" id="chart-container"></div>
