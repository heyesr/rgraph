A Pie chart with outline style highlighting

This form of highlighting originally used the tooltip RGraph event but has now been incorporated into the Pie chart and can be added to your own charts by setting the highlightStyle option to outline.

[No canvas support]

This goes in the documents header: 

<script src="RGraph.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.common.dynamic.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.common.tooltips.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.pie.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<canvas id="cvs" width="300" height="300">
    [No canvas support]
</canvas>
