A spline SVG Line chart

Here we have a spline Line chart with multiple datasets. The spline option simply makes the lines curvy instead of angular. There's also a shadow enabled and the vertical grid lines are disabled.

This goes in the documents header: 
<script src="RGraph.svg.common.core.js"></script>
<script src="RGraph.svg.line.js"></script>
Put this where you want the chart to show up: 
<div style="width: 750px; height: 300px" id="chart-container"></div>
